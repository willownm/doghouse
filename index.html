About Us

WHAT WE HAVE TO OFFER

We offer a refreshment area for your dogs that includes food, water, and treats. The Dog House also offers an Oops! Corner for our paw friends that could not afford their nature calling. This is to make your user experience helpful and relaxing.

The Dog House offers a fenced-backyard facilty for our paw friends with our family customers. You will be allowed to take your dogs inside to be your helper with shopping, or you can take it in the backyard for play time so shopping will not be stressful.

We know shopping can cause chaos and stress to our beloved customers. The Dog House wants you to feel more like family which is why we offer a complimentary refreshment fridge for our customers to make shopping a little easier and stress-free.

Testimony

WHY THE DOG HOUSE WAS FORM

Founded in 2017, the Dog House strives to provide everything you will need for your dogs. The owners Willow and Natalie gives their all into providing families with what they need for their four-legged family, and loving on the dogs that enter into the store. Their goal is to create an environment where families and their pets can come to get what they need and feel comfortable while doing so. They strive to make all people that come in the store feel at home and that everything they need for their dog is in their store. As pet owners, they know how important it is to feel like someone has your dogs’ best interest at heart when helping you find what you need. They love dogs and people, and they want to help in any way possible.


They want to give a big thanks to their family and friends because they have had to devote most of their time to the store. Natalie says, “We’ve had the amazing opportunity of seeing our store transition from a dream to a reality. The love and support we have gotten from everyone is the reason we’ve made it this far.” Willow adds on to their goal of the customers feeling like family by saying, “We not only want to provide the families with the material things for their dogs, but we also want to make it a place for these families and their pets to come and hangout.”
